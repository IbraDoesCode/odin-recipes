Ingredients

  • 4 pounds beef oxtails
  • 3 cups water
  • 2 beef bouillon cubes
  • 1/2 pound bok choy
  • 1/2 pound eggplant, cubed
  • 2 tablesppons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspons achiote poweder
  • 3 tablespoons smooth peanut butter

Steps

  1. Bring oxtails and water to a boil in a large pot. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, at least 2 hours, adding more water as necessary.
  2. Skim the fat from the pot at top of the liquid.
  3. Transfer oxtails to a plate. Crumble beef bouillon cubes into the pot and stir to dissolve.
  4. Add bok choy, beans, and eggplant and simmer until tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
  5. While the vegetables are simmering, heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until tender, about 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in achiote powder.
  7. Stir in peanut butter until melted.
  8. Add about 1/2 of the simmering broth and simmer for 5 minutes.
  9. Add oxtails and cook for 5 minutes.
  10. Transfer remaining broth and vegetables to the pot and stir to combine. Serve hot.
